# Pulse — Quick Start Guide

## What is Pulse?

Pulse is an AI Prospect Intelligence Platform built for IDBI Bank's Relationship Managers. It analyzes customer transaction data to predict loan needs before customers apply, delivering a prioritized lead queue with AI-generated explanations and conversation scripts.

---

## Running the App

### Option 1 — Python (no install needed)
```bash
cd "IDBI Hackathon/Pulse"
python3 -m http.server 8788
```
Then open: **http://localhost:8788**

### Option 2 — Node.js
```bash
cd "IDBI Hackathon/Pulse"
npx serve .
```
Then open the URL shown in the terminal.

### Option 3 — Direct file open
Double-click `index.html` — works for most screens, though relative asset paths work best when served.

## Screen-by-Screen Tour

### 1. RM Dashboard (`index.html`) — Start here
The morning command center for a Relationship Manager.
- **4 KPI tiles** — Hot Leads Today, Conversion Rate, Pipeline Value, AI Accuracy
- **Priority Lead Queue** — 8 top leads ranked by AI score, with life event badges
- **Live Signal Feed** — real-time transaction events triggering new leads
- **Weekly Performance chart** — leads generated vs converted

**Try:** Click "View all 47 leads →" to go to the Prospect List.

---

### 2. Prospect Intelligence (`src/views/lead-list.html`)
Full database of AI-scored prospects.
- **Filter sidebar** — narrow by life event, income range, signal recency
- **25-row table** — sortable, with AI scores, estimated income, free cash flow
- **Filter pills** — switch between loan types instantly

**Try:** Click "View" on any row to open the Customer 360 profile.

---

### 3. Customer 360 (`src/views/lead-detail.html`)
Deep intelligence report on Raj Kumar (demo customer).
- **Income Intelligence** — declared vs AI-estimated income (31% gap detected)
- **Affordability Engine** — free cash flow ₹57,400/mo, safe EMI ₹28,700
- **Life Event Evidence Trail** — 5 transaction signals confirming house shifting
- **RM Copilot Script** — ready-to-use conversation opener

**Try:** Click "Generate Full Offer" to go to the AI Offer Engine.

---

### 4. AI Offer Engine (`src/views/offer-engine.html`)
Explainability and offer configuration hub.
- **SHAP Feature Chart** — horizontal bars showing what drove the AI recommendation
- **5 Human-Readable Reasons** — pass/warn cards explaining the decision
- **Interactive Offer Builder** — drag the amount slider, watch EMI recalculate live
- **Compliance Footer** — RBI Fair Practices Code, DPDP Act 2023 verification

**Try:** Move the loan amount slider — the EMI updates instantly. Switch tenure between 3/5/7/10 years.

---

### 5. Mobile App (`src/views/mobile-app.html`)
What the customer sees in the IDBI mobile banking app.
- iPhone-frame mockup of the IDBI app
- Pre-approved offer card triggered by Pulse's AI detection
- "Why this offer?" accordion with customer-friendly signal explanations
